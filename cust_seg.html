Customer Segmentation

Project Information

  • Category : Machine Learning
  • Project Date : Aug, 2020
  • Project URL : https://github.com/Utkarsh217/Customer-Segmentation

What's the Project about!!!

Customer segmentation is the practice of dividing a customer base into groups of individuals that are similar in specific ways relevant to marketing, such as age, gender, interests and spending habits.

Problem Statement : An automobile company has plans to enter new markets with their existing products (P1, P2, P3, P4, P5). After intensive market research, theyâ€™ve deduced that the behavior of new market is similar to their existing market.

In their existing market, the sales team has classified all customers into 4 segments (A, B, C, D ). Then, they performed segmented outreach and communication for different segment of customers. This strategy has work exceptionally well for them. They plan to use the same strategy on new markets and have identified 2627 new potential customers. You are required to help the manager to predict the right group of the new customers.

Solution : To predict the right group of customers based on various factors such as Gender, Age, Profession, Graduation, Work Experience, Spending Score, Family Size of customers , built a machine learning model that can classify the segment type of each customer i.e whether they are of type A, B, C or D.

Libraries Used : Numpy, Pandas, Scikit-Learn
Data Visualization Tools : matplotlib, seaborn
Algorithm Used : LGBMClassifier
Evaluation Metric : Accuracy Score
